01
Select Material

Choose the panel material: Aluminium Alloy for lightweight structures, Stainless Steel for high-strength applications, or Carbon Composite for advanced aerospace components.

02
Choose Coating

Select your protective coating type. Anti-Corrosion uses polyurethane (MIL-PRF-85285). Thermal Protection applies ablative phenolic resin (MIL-PRF-14105E). Nano-Ceramic uses plasma-sprayed UHTC (ZrB₂/SiC).

03
Set Panel Size

Enter the panel width and height in metres. All preparation times, coating volumes, waste mass, and phase durations scale automatically to your panel area.

04
Run & Analyse

Click RUN SIMULATION to generate a full aerospace prep report: 5-phase timeline, coating intelligence, transfer efficiency, waste calculation, and simulation history.

Simulation data based on NASA TPS standards, MIL-PRF-85285, and MIL-PRF-14105E. Results model a single-applicator workflow — industrial operations use parallel teams for large panels.
